KrishiAI

AI-powered crop yield prediction and recommendation system using machine learning and environmental data analysis.

Problem Statement

Farmers rely on traditional methods for crop decisions, leading to inaccurate predictions. Existing systems consider limited parameters and fail to provide data-driven insights, resulting in low productivity and inefficient resource usage.

Research Gap & Solution

Most systems do not compare multiple machine learning models before prediction. KrishiAI solves this by evaluating different models and selecting the best one using performance metrics such as R² and MSE.
