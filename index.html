KrishiAI

KrishiAI is a machine learning based crop yield prediction and recommendation system that analyzes environmental factors such as temperature, rainfall, humidity, and soil conditions to help farmers make data-driven decisions for improved agricultural productivity.

Problem Statement

Agricultural decisions are still largely based on traditional knowledge and assumptions rather than data. Existing systems either provide static recommendations or rely on limited parameters, leading to inaccurate yield predictions and inefficient crop selection. This results in reduced productivity and economic loss for farmers.

Literature Review

Existing solutions include rule-based advisory systems and basic regression models. While they provide some level of prediction, they lack multi-feature integration and fail to capture complex relationships between environmental factors.

Research Gap & Solution

There is a lack of systems that compare multiple machine learning models and select the best one based on performance metrics. KrishiAI addresses this by evaluating multiple models and choosing the most accurate and efficient model for prediction.
