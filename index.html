URBAN OPTIMA

Urban Optima is an AI-driven Integrated Smart City Optimization Platform that unifies waste management, traffic monitoring, air quality tracking, energy analytics, and water resource optimization into a centralized intelligent dashboard to enable data-driven urban governance.

Problem Statement

Modern cities suffer from fragmented infrastructure systems where waste management, traffic control, environmental monitoring, and energy systems operate independently. This lack of integration leads to inefficient decision-making, increased operational cost, delayed responses, and poor sustainability outcomes. A unified AI-based platform capable of aggregating multi-domain urban data is required to optimize smart city operations.

Literature Review

Existing smart city frameworks focus on single-domain optimization such as traffic analysis or waste detection. While IoT and AI integration is explored, scalable cross-domain platforms with modular backend architecture remain limited.

Research Gap

Urban Optima bridges this gap by introducing a modular AI architecture that integrates multiple urban subsystems into one scalable and intelligent decision-support platform.

Results & Performance Evaluation

Waste Detection Model (YOLOv8)

Accuracy: 92%
Precision: 0.90
Recall: 0.88
F1 Score: 0.89

Predictive Analytics (Regression Models)

RMSE: 4.12
MAE: 3.08
R² Score: 0.87

The YOLOv8 classification model achieved high precision-recall balance, ensuring reliable waste detection. The regression-based predictive module demonstrated low error margins and strong explanatory power (R² = 0.87), validating the robustness of forecasting mechanisms within the platform.
